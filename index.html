About Me

I'm Ivan, a 24 years old musician and sound designer who is really passionate about videogames.

Since I was a little kid, I would study and try to understand how music and sounds were implemented in the games I played to make them so impactful and remarkable.

I studied Music & Sound Design at MOHOLE (MI) and obtained a degree in this field.

Being a musician, I also like to add melodic components to my sound design to maintain a narrative and obtain even more creative effects out of an image!
